                      An IELTS test for the community                    

                   

                      The IELTS is a high-stakes English proficiency test that is required for university admissions and professional licensing. The test is expensive and can be a                       barrier to access for many people. Our vision is to provide the IELTS at a low cost compared to the market, with a vision of spreading kindness and sharing                       logic. We believe that when we are kind to others and use logic to solve problems, we can create a better world for everyone.

How it works

           
             
               
                                 
               
                 

Step 1: Apply for the IELTS

                 

                    You can apply for the IELTS online. You will need to provide your personal information and pay the registration fee.                  

               
             
             
               
                                 
               
                 

Step 2: Take the IELTS

                 

                    You will need to take the IELTS at an official test center. The test consists of four sections: listening, reading, writing, and speaking.                  

               
             
             
               
                                 
               
                 

Step 3: Get your results

                 

                    Your results will be available online 13 days after the test date. You can view your scores and download a PDF copy of your Test Report Form.                  

               
             
             
               
                                 
               
                 

Step 4: Join the community

                 

                    Once you have your IELTS results, you can join our community. We have a Discord server where you can connect with other test-takers and get advice from our team                     of volunteers.
